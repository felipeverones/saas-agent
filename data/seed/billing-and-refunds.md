# Billing, plans and refunds

## Plans and pricing

Nimbus offers four plans, billed per seat, monthly or annually (annual saves 20%):

- **Free** — up to 5 members, 10 GB shared storage.
- **Pro** — $12/seat/month. 1 TB per member, offline mobile access, version
  history 90 days.
- **Business** — $24/seat/month. Unlimited storage, SSO/SAML, audit logs,
  version history 1 year, free guests.
- **Enterprise** — custom pricing. Dedicated infrastructure options, custom
  data retention, 99.9% uptime SLA, priority support.

## Upgrades, downgrades and proration

Upgrades take effect immediately; we charge a prorated amount for the remainder
of the current billing cycle. Downgrades take effect at the next renewal date —
we do not issue partial credits for unused time on downgrade. Seat count changes
are prorated the same way as plan changes.

## Payment methods and invoices

We accept credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) worldwide and ACH debit in the
US. Enterprise customers can pay by wire transfer with NET-30 terms. Invoices
are emailed to the billing contact and available under Settings > Billing >
Invoices. To change the billing email, the workspace owner must update it —
admins cannot.

## Refund policy

- Monthly subscriptions: refundable within **14 days** of the charge, once per
  customer per 12 months.
- Annual subscriptions: refundable within **30 days** of the charge.
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5-10 business days.
- Charges older than the window above are not refundable, but we may issue
  account credit at support's discretion for service quality issues.

**Approval requirements:** support agents may approve refunds up to $500
directly. Any refund **above $500 requires approval from a senior billing
specialist** (human review) before it is processed. Automated systems must
never issue refunds without this review step.

## Failed payments and dunning

If a charge fails, we retry on days 3, 7 and 14. The workspace enters read-only
mode after the second failed retry and is downgraded to Free after 30 days of
non-payment. Data is retained per the retention policy (see "Data export and
retention").
